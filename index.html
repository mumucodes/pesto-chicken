This easy recipe will knock out a dinner in about half an hour and will leave you feeling fed and full.

    Ingredients
  • 10 oz chopped chicken breast
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 Tbsp capers
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 4 oz orzo pasta
  • 4 oz grape tomatoes
  • 1/3 cup basil pesto
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 Tbsp dried currants
  • 2 Tbsp mayonnaise

    • Prepare Ingredients

    • Fill medium pot with salted water; cover and bring to a boil
    • Wash and dry produce
    • Peel the carrots; cut in half and cut into thin half circles
    • halve the tomatoes
    • Peel and rough chop the garlic
    • In a bowl, combine pesto and mayonnaise; add saltl and pepper to taste

    Cook the vegetables

    • In a medium nonstick pan heat 2tsp of olive oil on medium-high till hot
    • Add sliced carrots. Cook until lightly browned
    • Add tomatoes and garlic and cook for a couple more minutes
    • Transfer to a bowl

    Cook the pasta

    • In the meantime, add pasta to boiling water and cook 7-9 minutes, or until tender
    • Drain and return to pot

    Cook the chicken

    • In the meantime, pat chicken dry and season with salt and pepper
    • Heat oil on medium high
    • Add chicken and cook 3-4 minutes without turning and until lightly browned in color
    • Add currants, capers, and as much of the red pepper flakes as you wish. Cook for another 3-4 minutes
    • Remove from heat

    Finish and serve

    • To the pot of pasta, add the cooked vegetables, and drizzle of olive oil; stir and combine.
    • Taste, then season with additional salt and pepper if desired
    • Serve cooked chicken over pasta. Drizzle with creamy pesto and enjoy!
